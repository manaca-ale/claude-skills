### Logo Usage Rules

- **PDF header**: Use `logo_horizontal_tagline_light.png`, max height 1.2cm, left-aligned
- **PDF cover**: Use `logotype_light.png` or `symbol_light.png`, centered, max width 8cm
- **Dark background**: Use `_dark` or `_Monocromatico_Branco` variants
- **Minimum clear space**: 1x the height of the symbol on all sides
- **Never**: stretch, rotate, recolor, or place on low-contrast backgrounds
