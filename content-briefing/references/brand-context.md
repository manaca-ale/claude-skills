## Brand Voice Baseline

**Core voice**: Institucional, inspirador e confiante — but always accessible and human.

**Always**:
- Ground claims in real data and Manacá's actual experience
- Use "impacto socioambiental" (not just "impacto social")
- Reference territory, care, evidence, and systemic transformation
- Maintain B2B/B2G credibility while being warm
- Use Portuguese (PT-BR), formal-but-accessible register

**Never**:
- Invent statistics or metrics not verified in this document
- Use vague sustainability clichés ("salvar o planeta", "mudar o mundo", "revolucionando")
- Make commercial promises without evidence
- Use excessive technical jargon without explanation
- Confuse Manacá's consultancy with generic marketing agencies
